About Us

About Us

As a local energy company, PT Energi Bukit Delima helps the country by playing a role in procuring and selling High Speed Diesel (HSD), Marine Fuel Oil (MFO), Industrial Fuel Oil (IFO) with quality, integrity and sustainability, as well as leading the development of quality technology in the country.

PT Energi Bukit Delima, in its efforts to carry out downstream fuel oil business activities, with GEMA branding, also synergizes with Pertamina and industry.

We have adequate infrastructure to support the distribution and marketing of energy products. And we provide affordable energy for industries to grow and develop.

GEMA is ready to serve government and private companies in the oil and gas industry, shipping, mining and others.

Working Culture

At GEMA, we aim to continuously develop a proactive culture where safety and security are incorporated into everything we do.
We are committed to having a proactive safety culture towards people, the environment and the communities we are a part of.

Always Safe and High Value

The safety and security of our employees and the integrity of our operations are our top priorities. We believe that all accidents related to people, the environment and assets are preventable. We focus on the prevention of personal injuries, work-related illnesses and major accidents, and the goal of zero injuries has become part of the way we think and work, with a strong focus on continuous improvement.

Sustainability

For us, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. Our journey to grow as a broad energy company is based on a strong commitment to sustainability, and our strategy of always being safe, high value and low carbon is applied in everything we do.
